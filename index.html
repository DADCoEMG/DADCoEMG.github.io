Who are we?

DADCo is a rising biofeedback startup working in conjunction with Rochester Institute of Technology's Innovation Center. We seek to bring Electromyography (EMG) into the mainstream consumer marketplace.

What is EMG?

EMG stands for "Electromyography", a biodiagonstic technique for recording electrical activity produced by muscles. For example, a muscle being clenched will emit data similar to what is shown on the left. While currently only useful for medical and research purposes, DADCo seeks to utilize this technique in order to control consumer electronics.

Our Products

DADCo's flagship product will be an EMG-integrated smartwatch. Early prototypes have shown some very positive results and we're excited to make something great!
